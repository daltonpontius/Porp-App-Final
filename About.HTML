About Us

About us

PAZ Properties was founded in 2008 by Antonio Zizzo. We originally were meant to only host services for our parent company, Tailwind Inspections. SInce 2008 we have expanded and looked towards working with other companies and businesses as well. We provide three main services which are office leasing, residential leasing, and equipment rental. A large majority of our commercial office buildings available for lease are located in or near Tulsa, Oklahoma. We also provide a few residential leases near Tulsa, and a few leases tailored for students in Norman, Oklahoma. A large majority of our equipment rental is exclusive with Tailwind Inspection however for any business inquiries regarding equipment rental please feel free to contact us as we are willing to negotiate.

Our Mission

Provide the best quality commercial office spaces, residential leases, and equipment rental in Oklahoma.

Our Vision

We aspire to expand and begin working exclusively with many more corporations in Oklahoma in order to provide them with the best services.

Our Purpose

We aim to provide the best results and solve any issue our customers might have, we also aspire to keep up with advancing technology and keep our equipment rentals as up to date as possible.

Our Values

Honesty, Integrity, Humility, Respect for Others, Teamwork, Positive Attitude, Accountability, Stewardship, Loyalty

Tailwind Inspections

We started as a means to provide any and all assets for our parent company, Tailwind Inspections. We continue to do this to this day by providing them with the most up to date Non-Destructive Testing equipment available. We are constantly working with them to ensure that they are about to provide their services on time and in an efficient manner. Their services are provided globally so our equipment is forced to travel with them as well. They are the best in their industry and aspire to improve which we here at PAZ Properties admire and try to mimic.
