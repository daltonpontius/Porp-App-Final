PAZ Properties

PAZ Properties is a property and equipment management company located in Tulsa, Oklahoma. PAZ Properties owns multiple office buildings as well residential homes and condominium apartments. These properties are located all throughout Oklahoma, with a majority of them residing near Tulsa Oklahoma.

Parent Company

Tailwind Inspection is a provider of non-destructive testing services to the aircraft and aerospace industries. With over 150 years of combined experience, we have one of the highest quality trained staffs in the industry. The aircraft industry is an ever changing and challenging business. Rules, regulations, certification requirements and new equipment and procedures require a dedication to the business. PAZ Properties works with Tailwind Inspections regulary to provide any and all equipment they need, while also providing all of their commercial work space.
