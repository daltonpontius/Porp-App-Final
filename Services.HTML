Our Services

At PAZ Properties our main focus is to serve as a landlord/property owner for commercial office buildings in Oklahoma. We also offer residential rental properties and equipment rentals by request. To learn more about our services feel free to send us an email as we'll try to get back to you as soon as possible.

Office Leasing

PAZ Properties is a leading commercial office space rental company in Tulsa, oklahoma. We offer multiple properties near downtown Tulsa that have plenty of office space to accommodate for your large or small business. We currently do not have any commercial office spaces available but will update our website regularly when these spaces do become available.

Residental Leasing

We currently own a few residential rental properties in Norman, Oklahoma. These yearly leases are focused on the school year and are aimed for parents or students looking for a place to live that is close to the OU Campus. All amenities are included in these leases and it is based on a per room lease rather than per unit. For more information please feel free to email us any question regarding these residential leases.

Equipment Rental

PAZ Properties’s parent company, Tailwind Inspections, is one of the world leading companies in the Non-Destructive Testing industry. They serve major airline companies around the world and have been in business for over two decades. Through PAZ Properties we offer equipment rental services to Tailwind Inspections exclusively. All of the equipment used by Tailwind Inspections is owned and managed by PAZ Properties.
